WELCOME

    Greetings to you and WELCOME to the official website of MESRA (MES Raja Residential School & Jr. College Alumni Association) Kallanthode, NIT Kozhikode,India. Ever since we left the school we have not had an organized effort for reunion except for a few casual meetings from which nothing much was materialised. This was the reason behind the formation of this association with the support of a lot of our old friends from around the world as an earnest attempt to get back to our old class mates and friends. We are grateful to a lot of people who have been very kind enough and took a lot of pain to initiate this venture and keep it going, in spite of their busy schedules. We seek your support, help and guidance on all fronts. This is just a small beginning and we have got a long way ahead of us..
